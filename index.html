#002 - THE TITAN

Born in the fires of a legendary market surge, this unstoppable force is a reward for unwavering conviction. The BullDuck is destined to be held by the most bullish and steadfast investors, a trophy signifying their faith. Legends say its roar can rally a floor price from the brink of collapse, making its holder a revered oracle of prosperity and the sworn protector of the Sanctuary's treasury.

The Hunter

#003 - THE SEEKER

A silent predator of the open market, The Hunter cannot be tamed or owned. He is the ultimate treasure seeker, a wild anomaly that stalks the marketplace floor, hunting for undervalued and forgotten ducks. His rifle fires not bullets, but swift, decisive bids, sweeping the rarest finds into his legendary collection. He appears when a treasure is revealed and vanishes once his prize is secured, a guardian of value that answers only to the thrill of the hunt.
